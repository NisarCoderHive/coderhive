We are CoderHive

We make it easy for people to launch their career in Technology Startups

CoderHive has been started by Khaja Naquiuddin and Nisar Ahmed Khan to help students get their dream job.

Khaja while working in Startups mentored lot of students to get job as Software Developer. While doing this he observed that there are large number of students who need proper mentoring and guidance to get a professional job. Most students are struggling and unaware of jobs in the market. They have no idea how to prepare for them. Especially students from semi-urban and rural areas.
​
On other side it was disturbing for both Khaja and Nisar to see some talented students resorting to low salary jobs and not earning to their worth. After coming across all these kind of problems both Nisar and Khaja decided to start CoderHive to help students get into tech companies where they do their dream job and grow as a successful professional

Khaja Naquiuddin

CEO and Co-Founder

Khaja has been working in startups and product based companies for more than 7 years. He consulted for companies in India, US, Canada and Germany and has lot of experience in Software Development and in mentoring students and interns. His passion for education and strong belief in learning for mastery and to make responsible citizens who contribute for the growth of society and world, has enabled him to start CoderHive. Apart from mentoring, he does academic research and takes care of placements

Nisar Ahmed Khan

COO and Co-Founder

Nisar Ahmed Khan has been teaching programming to students for more than 14 years. He is one of the most liked programming teachers. His students are successfully working in tech companies.After learning how current college education is outdated for the real world, he realised his passion to impart quality education and help students for a better career can be fulfilled through CoderHive. So he quit his college job and declined other lucrative university jobs to join CoderHive full time.
